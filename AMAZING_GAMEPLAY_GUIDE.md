# 🏡 Township 2D - Player's Manual & Gameplay Guide 🌾🐮🚂

Welcome to your new Town! As the Mayor, your goal is to build, farm, produce goods, fulfill orders, and manage railway logistics to grow your town. 

Here is your complete guide on how the game works and how to play it!

---

## 🎮 Basic Controls & Navigation

*   **Move Camera:** Make sure the **Select (Pointer 👆)** tool is active in the bottom toolbar, then click and drag the grass map to pan around.
*   **Zoom In/Out:** Scroll your mouse wheel up to zoom in and down to zoom out.
*   **Select Tools:** Click any tool in the bottom dock to make it active.
*   **Sound Toggle:** Click the sound button in the top-left corner to mute or unmute the game sounds.
*   **Reset Progress:** Click the red **Reset Game** button in the bottom-left corner if you want to wipe your progress and start your town from scratch.

---

## 🌾 1. Farming & Harvesting Crops
Your fields are the starting point of your entire economy:
1.  Select a seed tool from the bottom dock: **Wheat** 🌾, **Corn** 🌽, **Carrot** 🥕, or **Sugarcane** 🍭.
2.  Click and drag (swipe) your cursor across empty brown soil plots to plant them.
3.  **Growth Stages:** Watch them grow from tiny green sprouts 🌱 into fully grown stalks. When ripe:
    *   **Wheat** displays golden sways.
    *   **Corn** shows green leaves and yellow cobs.
    *   **Carrot** reveals orange carrot tops peeking out of the dirt.
    *   **Sugarcane** stands as tall segmented bamboo-like stalks.
4.  Select the **Harvest (Scissors ✂️)** tool and swipe across the ripe crops to harvest them into your Barn.

---

## 🐮 2. Feding & Milking Cows
Keep your cows fed to collect fresh milk:
1.  Select the **Feed Cow (Cow Feed 🐮🌾)** tool from the bottom toolbar.
2.  Click and swipe across the cows wandering inside the fenced Pasture yard.
3.  **Digestion:** Fed cows will stand still and chew on a yellow hay stack. A green progress bar and timer (e.g. `EATING 20s`) will count down above their backs.
4.  **Milking:** Once finished, they will walk around again and display a milk bottle bubble `🥛`. Select the **Select (Pointer 👆)** tool and click on the cow to collect the milk.

---

## 🏭 3. Manufacturing in Factories
Factories process raw crops into premium foods and animal feed. Click on any factory using the **Select (Pointer 👆)** tool to open its panel:
*   **🌾 Feed Mill:**
    *   *Cow Feed:* Costs 2 Wheat (takes 10s).
    *   *Corn Meal:* Costs 2 Corn (takes 20s).
*   **🍞 Bakery:**
    *   *Bread:* Costs 2 Wheat (takes 15s).
    *   *Cake:* Costs 2 Wheat + 1 Sugar (takes 45s).
*   **🥛 Dairy Factory:**
    *   *Butter:* Costs 1 Cow Feed (takes 30s).
    *   *Cheese:* Costs 1 Corn Meal (takes 40s).
*   **🍬 Sugar Refinery:**
    *   *Sugar:* Costs 2 Sugarcane (takes 25s).
    *   *Syrup:* Costs 3 Sugarcane (takes 50s).

*Once production is complete, click the floating product bubble above the factory's chimney to harvest the item.*

---

## 🚁 4. Helicopter Pad (Citizen Orders)
Fulfilling citizen orders is your main source of Gold and Experience (XP):
1.  Click the **Helipad** (circular concrete pad marked "H") using the **Select (Pointer 👆)** tool.
2.  Review the orders board. Fulfilling an order immediately replaces it with a new one.
3.  **🎓 Hover Helper:** If you don't know how to get an item, **hover your mouse over the client's name** (e.g. `Officer Marie ❓`). An animated guide will pop up showing exactly how to grow or manufacture each ingredient!
4.  Click the green **SEND** button to dispatch the helicopter. It will fly off-screen and return after 12 seconds with your rewards.
5.  *Tip: Click the trashcan `🗑️` to discard an order. A new one will replace it in 20 seconds.*

---

## 🚂 5. Train Logistics & Building Materials
The three railway tracks at the top of the screen host three different cargo trains. Click on any track using the **Select (Pointer 👆)** tool to open its depot:
*   **🚅 Express Cargo Train (Track 0):** Has 4 cargo crates. Fast travel (**12s**). Demands refined items. Returns double quantities of construction materials.
*   **Local Freighter Train (Track 1):** Has 3 cargo crates. Normal travel (**25s**). Demands basic crops. Returns standard materials.
*   **🚇 Metro Materials Train (Track 2):** Has 5 cargo crates. Slow travel (**45s**). Demands bulk quantities. Returns massive shipments of materials.
*   **🔄 Recycle Cargo:** If a train demands items you cannot make, click the **🔄 Recycle Cargo** button in the header to re-roll and refresh all requests!
*   **How to dispatch:** Load all crates (displays the Coins/XP reward for each) and click **SEND TRAIN**. Collect construction materials (Bricks `🧱`, Glass `🥛`, Slabs `🪨`) when the train returns.

---

## 📦 6. Barn Storage & Upgrading Capacity
All harvested crops and products go into your Barn:
1.  Click the `📦 BARN` resource pill in the top-right corner to open your inventory.
2.  **Selling Items:** If your Barn is full, click the `🪙 Sell` button on any item card to trade it for coins and free up storage.
3.  **Upgrades:** Collect Bricks, Slabs, and Glass from returning cargo trains. Click the **Upgrade Barn** button to permanently increase your storage by **+20 slots**.

---

## 🏗️ 7. Shop & Building Placements
1.  Click the **Shop (Crane 🏗️)** button on the bottom toolbar.
2.  Select an item to buy (e.g., Crop Plots, Cowshed, Bakery, Dairy, Sugar Refinery). 
    *   *Note: High-level factories like the Dairy and Sugar Refinery unlock at levels 2 and 3.*
3.  Move your mouse over the grass grid. The placement box turns Green if the space is clear and Red if it overlaps with another building or the train tracks.
4.  Click on any green spot to buy and place the building!

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Mayor Success
*   **Prioritize Train Cargo:** returning cargo trains are the *only* way to get construction materials for upgrading your Barn capacity.
*   **Utilize Hover Guides:** Hover over Helipad client names whenever you need a quick recipe check.
*   **Re-roll Hard Crates:** Don't hesitate to use the **🔄 Recycle Cargo** button in the train modal if a train is stuck on a recipe you don't want to make.
*   **Keep Your Pastures Fed:** Milk is a bottleneck ingredient for Butter and Cheese. Keep your cows eating!
